SECTION 1: ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

The following eligibility criteria are MANDATORY and must be fulfilled by every bidder. Failure to meet any mandatory criterion will result in disqualification.

1.1 FINANCIAL ELIGIBILITY

1.1.1 Minimum Annual Turnover
The bidder must have an average annual turnover of not less than Rs. 5,00,00,000 (Rupees Five Crore) from construction activities during the last three consecutive financial years (FY 2021-22, FY 2022-23, FY 2023-24), as evidenced by audited balance sheets certified by a Chartered Accountant.

1.1.2 Solvency / Net Worth
The bidder must have a positive net worth as per the latest audited balance sheet. Negative net worth at any point during the last three years will render the bid non-compliant.

1.1.3 Bid Security (EMD)
An Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) of Rs. 84,00,000 (Rupees Eighty-Four Lakh) must be submitted in the form of a Demand Draft or Bank Guarantee from a scheduled commercial bank in favour of "DG CRPF, New Delhi".

---

1.2 TECHNICAL ELIGIBILITY

1.2.1 Similar Work Experience
The bidder must have successfully completed at least 3 (three) similar construction projects in the last 5 (five) years (i.e., on or after 01 April 2019), where each project had a contract value of not less than Rs. 1,00,00,000 (Rupees One Crore) individually. "Similar work" means construction of residential/institutional buildings or barracks, roads, or civil infrastructure for government/PSU/defence organisations. Certificates from the competent authority of the respective client organisation must be provided.

1.2.2 Key Technical Personnel
The bidder must deploy a team including at minimum: one Graduate Civil Engineer with at least 5 years of post-qualification experience and one Diploma-level Supervisor with at least 3 years of site experience. CVs and proof of qualification must be submitted.

---

1.3 COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS

1.3.1 GST Registration
The bidder must hold a valid Goods and Services Tax (GST) registration certificate under the CGST Act, 2017. The GST registration number must be provided and must be currently active.

1.3.2 ISO 9001:2015 Certification
The bidder must hold a valid ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System certification issued by a NABCB-accredited certification body. The certificate must be valid on the date of submission of the bid.

1.3.3 EPF and ESIC Registration
The bidder must be registered under the Employees' Provident Fund and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1952 (EPF) and the Employees' State Insurance Act, 1948 (ESIC). Registration numbers must be provided.

---

1.4 DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS

1.4.1 Registration with CPWD / State PWD / CRPF
The bidder must be registered as a contractor with CPWD, any State Public Works Department, CRPF, or any central government / defence organisation. A valid contractor registration certificate with class and category details must be submitted.

1.4.2 PAN Card
A valid Permanent Account Number (PAN) card issued by the Income Tax Department of India is mandatory.

---

SECTION 2: EVALUATION METHODOLOGY

Bids will be evaluated in two stages:
Stage 1: Technical and Eligibility Evaluation (Pass/Fail)
Stage 2: Financial Bid Opening (for eligible bidders only)

CRPF reserves the right to verify any document submitted by the bidder directly with the issuing authority. Any misrepresentation will result in immediate disqualification and may attract penal action.

---

SECTION 3: SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS

Bids must be submitted online through the GeM portal by 17:00 hours on the last date of submission. Physical documents as listed in the document checklist must be submitted in sealed envelope to the address above before the deadline.

All documents must be self-attested. Translated copies of documents in regional languages must be accompanied by a certified English translation.

---

SECTION 4: SCOPE OF WORK (Summary)

Construction of:
- 4 barracks blocks (G+2 structure, 40 rooms each)
- 1 administrative building
- Internal roads and drainage
- Water supply and sewerage infrastructure
- Electrical works including DG set provision
- Perimeter boundary wall

Completion period: 18 months from date of work order.
